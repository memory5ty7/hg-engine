## Personal Changes

### New weathers
New weathers effects have been added to the game :
- 5 turns tailwind for the enemy
- Permanent tailwind for the enemy
- Stealth rocks on your field
- 3 layers of spikes on your field
- 2 layers of toxic spikes on your field
- Permanent sandstorm + Stealth rocks on your field
- Electric terrain (no effect)
- Grassy terrain (no effect)
- Psychic terrain (no effect)
- Misty terrain (no effect)
- Sticky Web (placeholder - not working yet)

The ID of all the weathers is accessible and modifiable [here](include/constants/weather_numbers.h).\
These new weathers do not have a visual effect on the overworld and do not have any animation in battle yet.
